Haskell Now! announced

HaskellNow is conference dedicated to spread the joy and love of the Haskell programming language. We hope to create unique learning environment, which both beginners and advanced Haskellers will find informative

We've managed to round up an amazing line-up of speakers (soon to be announced) that will guarantee both amazing lecture content as well as instructive networking environment. Conference is devided into two tracks: beginners and advanced. This way this event becomes inviting for both people who are new or intrigued with the language as well developers who are familiar with it and hope to extand their understanding.

HaskellNow will take place in Wroc≈Çaw (Poland), which is a city of culture, education and technology. Almost 90 tech meetups gather around passionate and experts from various fields. In 2018 we want to turn Wroclaw into a purely functional city!

Every initiative needs a support, because no man is an island. We believe that together we can make Haskell Now happen. By cooperation with companies who care by a tech community, we are going to move Haskell world. And we are going to do it Now! If you happen to know a company or organization that would be willing to help us with the event, please let us know!
